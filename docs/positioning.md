# Synapse Positioning

Use this language for pilot conversations, README copy, demos, and release notes.

## Approved Positioning

- Synapse is a webcam-first, local-first focus and fatigue support tool.
- Synapse helps users review broad attention patterns during a session.
- Synapse processes webcam landmarks locally on the user's machine.
- Synapse does not intentionally save raw webcam video or still images.
- Synapse saves local calibration values, session metrics, alert logs, and text reports.
- Synapse is pilot software for quality learning before any billing or paid deployment.
- Synapse outputs should be interpreted with participant feedback and environmental context.

## Approved Use Cases

- Individual self-review after a work or study session.
- Internal 5-person no-payment pilot.
- Webcam, onboarding, report, and replay quality testing.
- Debugging metric behavior across lighting and camera setups.
- Gathering consent, privacy, and product comprehension feedback.

## Prohibited Claims

Do not claim that Synapse:

- Diagnoses fatigue, ADHD, burnout, depression, stress, anxiety, or any medical condition.
- Detects truth, deception, intent, motivation, or trustworthiness.
- Measures real emotions as facts.
- Scores employee productivity, performance, loyalty, or effort.
- Is validated for hiring, grading, legal, safety-critical, insurance, or disciplinary decisions.
- Works accurately across all faces, lighting, cameras, glasses, skin tones, ages, or environments.
- Stores no data at all. The accurate claim is that raw webcam video is not intentionally saved, while local metrics and reports are saved.

## Short Description

Synapse is a local Windows pilot app that uses webcam landmarks to estimate broad focus, fatigue, and distraction patterns during a session, then creates local reports for review.

## Demo Disclaimer

Use this disclaimer in demos:

> Synapse is pilot software. It processes webcam landmarks locally and saves local session metrics and reports, but it does not intentionally save raw video. Its outputs are noisy indicators for review, not medical, emotional, productivity, or disciplinary conclusions.
